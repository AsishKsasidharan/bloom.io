Physical Oceanography · Bay of Bengal

Bay of Bengal Lab for Ocean Observation and Modelling

BLOOM investigates the physical, biological, and chemical dynamics of the Bay of Bengal using ocean observations, Argo profiling floats, satellite remote sensing, and regional ocean modelling frameworks including CROCO.
